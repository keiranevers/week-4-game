Crystals Collector!!

Match the Random Number

Game Objectives

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. By clicking on a crystal, you will add a speific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number, you lose the game if your total score results in a higher number than the random number given.

The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it. The number assigned to the crystal will remain the same during the instance of the game.

Each time the game starts, the game will change the values of the Randon number, as well as the value of each crystal.
